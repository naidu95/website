The Experience

At Alekhya Homes, we are happy to say that we have built our business on honesty, integrity and attention to detail. We believe that the moment someone steps into the gate of an apartment complex or a house, they need to be transported into a space that is at once welcoming, comforting and distinct. The Alekhya Experience is characterized by complete adherence to our design values and principles. We believe in creating homes whose beauty is marked by simplicity, derived from nature and above-all is organic in architecture. Each home we build is characterized by sensitivity to site and structure. Our design values and principles guide us in every project we take up and ensure that the outcome is always delightful to our clients

Traditional

We draw tremendous value and learning from traditional Indian architecture. Whether is it detailing nooks and corners within homes such as pergolas and urulis, or the kind of finish we give to the wood and stone we use in construction, the ultimate form of our design is distinctively traditional.

Simple

We believe that beauty and utility need not be overstated and so, our buildings are characterized by clean lines and minimalism lending a fresh and contemporary look to them. A lot of times, our decisions of whether to choose a design or not boils down to whether it is simple enough or not.

Natural

Our homes derive their sense of design and colors from nature and are also built in harmony with nature. Irrespective of where you are standing in one of our constructions, your eyes will see a part of nature. Our homes are punctuated with organic living plants that are native to the region.

Customizable

We understand that each of our clients has a different vision for their dream home. We work very closely with each client to understand their specific requirements and translate them into architectural forms that are both aesthetic and functional. Ultimately, people become extensions of their homes and vice versa.
